World Travel

Growing up the son of an airline pilot, I was very fortunate to have the opportunity to travel to different parts of the world from a young age. Whenever I traveled with my father he would always make sure I fully engaged myself with the local culture. From the age of six, he would always make me sit on my own whenever traveling on public transportation. He stayed nearby to keep a watchful eye, but it forced me to become comfortable striking up conversations with those around me. These experiences instilled in me a love of traveling to different parts of the world to experience new cultures and get to know individuals. I love meeting new people, whether it be a simple chat about a sporting event or a deep conversation about their life - making human connections and positive impacts on other people's life is the most important thing to me.
