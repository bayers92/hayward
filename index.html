World Travel

As the son of a pilot I was fortunate enough to have the opportunity to travel to different parts of the world from a young age. My family encouraged my passion to fully engage with local cultures wherever I found myself. This allowed me to broaden my horizons and catch glimpses of the world through other people’s eyes. These experiences instilled in me a love of traveling to different parts of the world to experience new cultures and to get to know individuals. I love meeting new people, whether it be a simple chat about a sporting event or a deep conversation about their life - making human connections and positive impacts on other people's lives is the most important thing to me.
